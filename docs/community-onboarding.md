# Community Onboarding

This guide defines onboarding steps for new community contributors.

## Onboarding Goals

- Welcome contributors quickly
- Provide clear starting points
- Reduce friction for first contributions

## New Contributor Path

1. Read governance overview documents.
2. Join official community channels.
3. Review contribution guidelines.
4. Pick a starter task or propose an improvement.
5. Submit a contribution and request feedback.

## Maintainer Support

Maintainers should:

- Respond respectfully and clearly
- Suggest next actions
- Share relevant templates and examples

## Success Signals

- First contribution completed
- Contributor remains active after first month
- Contributor understands escalation and conduct policies
