# Leadership Roles

This document provides a role-level summary of OSSAfrica governance
leadership responsibilities.

For detailed assignments and current role mapping, see
[current_leadership.md](current_leadership.md).

## Role Categories

- Strategic leadership
- Operational leadership
- Community leadership
- Technical and security leadership

## Common Expectations

- Active participation and responsiveness
- Transparent communication
- Contributor support and mentorship
- Adherence to governance and conduct policies
