# Community Call Playbook

This playbook standardizes planning and running OSSAfrica community calls.

## Pre-Call Checklist

- Define objective and agenda
- Confirm host and note taker
- Share invite and reminders
- Prepare links and references

## During Call

- Start with welcome and conduct reminder
- Follow agenda and time boxes
- Capture actions, owners, and deadlines
- Reserve time for open Q&A

## Post-Call

- Publish meeting notes
- Share action items and owners
- Track unresolved follow-ups

## Templates

Use templates in [../templates](../templates) for recurring call operations.
